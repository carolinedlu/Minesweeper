HOW TO PLAY

You have a rectangular field of buttons. Under some of these buttons are mines.

If you left click a button covering a mine, you lose and the game is over.

If you left click a button that is adjacent to at least one mine, the button is replaced with a label indicating the number of adjacent mines.

If the square is not adjacent to any mines, the adjacent buttons that have not already been clicked are automatically pressed.

You can mark a button as a mine by shift left-clicking on the button.

A marked button does not do anything if left-clicked.

The game ends when you clear/left-click all of the spaces that do not contain mines and mark all of the spaces with mines.

If you left-click on cleared spaces that have a label indicating adjacent bombs:

If the number of adjacent spaces that have been marked as bombs equals the number indicated by the label, adjacent spaces that have not been cleared and are not marked as bombs will be automatically left-clicked.

If the number of adjacent spaces that have been marked as bombs does not equal the number indicated by the label, nothing happens.
